About Us

Welcome to Society Welfare! Lets join hands together to make the society a better one.
Society Welfare is a non-profit organisation which helps people to collaborate for a public cause. People can

contribute and volunteer for a particular job to be done like cleaning a garbage dump. has a rewards system in

site coins for completing a work. People can gain respect for contributing or volunteering for a particular work. The

rewards system encourages people to do more work for the public which has been abandoned by the according

government official or which is inaccessible by the municipality corporation.
